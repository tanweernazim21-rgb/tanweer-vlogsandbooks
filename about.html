About the Author

A professional journey in IT. A creative journey in writing.

Syed Tanweer Islam is an Indian author and IT Delivery Manager based in Bangalore with over 22 years of experience in the IT industry.

His professional work spans service desk operations, delivery management, customer experience, incident management, global support operations, people leadership, and operational excellence.

Through fiction, he explores love, memory, family, sacrifice, and the choices that define human lives. Through non-fiction, he shares leadership lessons for IT professionals growing from support roles into leadership responsibilities.

Blue Quill Studio was created as a home for books, stories, leadership ideas, and future creative work.
